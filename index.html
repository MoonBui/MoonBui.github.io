A few things I did that made me proud

My first coding project

My first ever coding project was a game I made in highschool using Game Maker 2. I liked video games, so I tried coding one. Needless to say, it's not the best individual game project out there. The mechanics were simple, the art looks like a five-year-old genius made it, however I'm still proud of it. I came in not knowing anything about coding, and came out knowing how to google for help with coding (and also wanting to code more).

Ichigo Ichie - an unforgettable 2018

This is my proudest achievement even to this day. Working in the board of directors for our school's Japanese-Vietnamese club, I dedicated 6 months of preparation to organize one festival day celebrating the cultural stories and folk tales of Japan and Vietnam. The festival, despite being the first of its kind to be fully organized by highschoolers, was a huge success. Attracting around 2000 participants, with a revenue of approximately $1500 made from selling $0.75/ticket.
